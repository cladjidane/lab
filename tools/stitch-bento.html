PAI

L'IA qui se souvient

Personal AI is an experiment in augmenting memory. It leverages vector databases to create a searchable, queryable extension of your digital history.

Unlike general LLMs, PAI focuses on precision and source attribution, ensuring every generated thought is anchored in your actual context.
